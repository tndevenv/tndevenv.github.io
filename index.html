Ten Dao-Nguyen

Software engineer with experience in mobile, desktop, and web applications and services. Specializing in full-stack development and responsive design.

Learning with intentional abandon.

Back in 2012, I built my first console application that generated a pun from a collection of puns the pun-derful people in my life would share. It led me to dive head-first into the wonders of this craft known as software development.

Fast forward to today, and I’ve had the privilege of building software for start-ups up to large corporations within industries ranging from data management, health and wellness, financial technology, identity theft/prevention, public safety, and international standards.

My main focus these days is to develop software meant to change the world, however big or small the impact. I love building software for the plethora of communities that exist and involving these people with an array of lived experiences into the process of a solutions design.

When I’m not at the computer, I’m usually reading, embroidering, spending time with my partner and our 2 dogs, or cycling aimlessly while enjoying the outdoors.
